Welcome to HackCU!

HackCU 2016 is the yearly 36 hour hackathon at the Idea Forge on the beautiful campus of the University of Colorado Boulder. HackCU brings together people of all experience levels to give them an opportunity to express their creativity, ingenuity, and determination to build something amazing.
