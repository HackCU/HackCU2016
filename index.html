Welcome to HackCU!

HackCU hosts the largest annual 36 hour hackathon in the Rocky Mountain region at the University of Colorado Boulder. We bring together people of all experience levels, from all over the country, to give you an opportunity to express your creativity, ingenuity, and determination to build something amazing. This event will help inspire and empower you to do the things you never knew you could.

FAQs


What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is an event where people come together and use technology to transform ideas into reality. You meet awesome new people, build something amazing from scratch, and you learn a ton of new things. Hackathon's are generally 12-36 hours long filled with food, activities, caffeine, and sometimes competitions and prizes.


What should I bring?

Laptop
Chargers
Toiletries
A change of clothes
Pillow
Headphones


Will there be food?


Yes. Lots. We will be providing three meals a day as well as delicious snacks, drinks, and caffeine.


How much does it cost?


Nada. Nothing. Zero. HackCU is complete free, and if you are traveling from outside of Boulder, we will even reimburse your travel. Contact us for more details.


What about sleep?


There will be so much activity going on that you probably won't want to sleep! We are working on getting a place to sleep, however.


Can I get involved if I'm not a student?


Of course! We always need sponsors, mentors, and help setting and cleaning up. Contact us for more details.


Who can apply?


Any current college student who will be 18 years old or older when HackCU begins(March 18th-20th).


Do I need a team?


Not at all! You can come in with friends, but you will meet awesome people and making a team at the event will be facilitated.
