April 1st - 3rd, 2016

HackCU hosts the largest annual 36 hour hackathon in the Rocky Mountain region at the University of Colorado Boulder. This year, April 1st through 3rd, we are bringing together over 250 students to give you an opportunity to express your creativity, ingenuity, and determination to build something amazing.
