Welcome to HackCU!

HackCU hosts the largest annual 36 hour hackathon in the Rocky Mountain region at the University of Colorado Boulder. This year, we are bringing together people of all experience levels from all over the country to give you an opportunity to express your creativity, ingenuity, and determination to build something amazing. HackCU is here to inspire and empower you to do the things you never thought you could.
