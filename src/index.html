Welcome to HackCU!

HackCU hosts the largest annual 36 hour hackathon in the Rocky Mountain region at the University of Colorado Boulder. This year, we are bringing together people of all experience levels from all over the country to give you an opportunity to express your creativity, ingenuity, and determination to build something amazing.

FAQs


What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is an event where people come together and use technology and creativity to transform ideas into a reality. You meet awesome new people, build something amazing from scratch, and learn new things.



What should I bring?

  • Laptop

  • Chargers

  • A change of clothes

  • Toiletries

  • Headphones

  • Pillow



Will there be food?

Yes. Lots. We will be providing three meals a day as well as delicious snacks, drinks, and caffeine.



How much does it cost?

Nothing! HackCU is complete free. Unfortunately we will not be able to offer any travel reimbursement for HackCU 2016. Contact us for more details.



Who can apply?

Any current college student who will be 18 years old or older when HackCU begins (April 1, 2016).
